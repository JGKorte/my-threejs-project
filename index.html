Just a weekend coding project making a browser based editor for my glb/gltf files.
Click on parts of the model to select, use the delete key on your keyboard to remove and ctrl+z to undo if you deleted something by mistake
Click and drag to orbit the camera, middle mouse to zoom and right-click to pan
On the top-right you'll find a menu to swap models and a button to export an STL file, but for some reason the exported model is tiny
so you'll need to scale it 100000% in your slicer to be the correct size. Not sure why *shrug*
